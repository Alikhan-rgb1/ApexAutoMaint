Frequently Asked Questions

How does concierge pickup work? +
Call or WhatsApp us. We'll pick up your vehicle from home, office, or anywhere. While we repair, you get a loaner car. Once done, we deliver it back to you. Free service across UAE.
Are you insurance approved? +
Yes. We're authorized for AXA, Allianz, ADIB, Emirates Insurance, and all major UAE insurers. Direct billing available. We handle all paperwork for you.
What warranty do you provide? +
3-year warranty on all repairs and service work. Parts come with manufacturer warranties. We're ISO 9001 certified. All work is 100% guaranteed.
How much does ceramic coating cost? +
Ceramic coating typically costs 1,500-4,000 AED depending on vehicle size. PPF costs 3,000-8,000 AED. Both protect your paint. Get a free quote—call us.
Do you work across all emirates? +
Yes. We provide concierge service across Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Abu Dhabi, and all UAE emirates. Free pickup and delivery throughout.
How often should I service my car? +
Typically every 10,000-15,000 km or annually. In UAE's harsh climate, we recommend 8,000 km intervals. We'll create a personalized maintenance schedule for you.
